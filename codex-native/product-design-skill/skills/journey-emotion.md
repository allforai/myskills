# Journey Emotion For Codex Native

Use this workflow to map emotional highs, lows, and risk points across real
user journeys.

## Objectives

- identify critical journey moments
- capture frustration, trust, and recovery points
- inform downstream experience design

## Outputs

- journey and emotion artifacts under `.allforai/`

## Completion standard

- key journeys have emotional shape and recovery notes
- critical risks are surfaced explicitly
