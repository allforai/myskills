# Product Map For Codex Native

Use this workflow to turn the concept into structured product artifacts.

## Objectives

- identify roles, tasks, flows, constraints, and patterns
- produce the machine-readable map that downstream workflows consume

## Outputs

- `.allforai/product-map/role-profiles.json`
- `.allforai/product-map/task-inventory.json`
- `.allforai/product-map/business-flows.json`

## Completion standard

- task inventory exists and is non-empty
- roles and business flows are traceable
