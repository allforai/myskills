# Design Audit For Codex Native

Use this workflow to perform cross-layer consistency and traceability checks on
the finished design artifacts.

## Objectives

- verify reverse traceability
- verify coverage across maps, journeys, and UI
- identify cross-layer inconsistencies

## Outputs

- `.allforai/design-audit/audit-report.json`
- `.allforai/design-audit/audit-report.md`

## Completion standard

- audit artifacts exist
- major inconsistencies are explicit and actionable
